Ars Futura

I co-founded Ars Futura with a friend back in 2013 when I was 22 years old. Today, Ars Futura has 24 employees and we have worked with more than 30 clients from all around the world, building mobile and web apps.

Our clients range from small startups, established agencies to large corporations. We have worked with Activision Blizzard and their subsidiary Major League Gaming to bring esports before millions of people around the world. With a little help from us, Elemental Machines is revolutionizing science-based industries with powerful, data-driven insights. We partnered with MITURF to build the best way to discover and book sporting spaces. And The Pigeonhole is bringing serialized reading back onto the mobile phones of modern people.

You can find out more about Ars Futura and our clients at our website.
