Pincone

Pincone is a simple and well-designed bookmarking tool that makes it easier to share content with others. By signing up, you get access to your Personal Pincone for free where you can bookmark links for yourself. You can also create teams with your coworkers, family, friends and share links with each other.

You can read more about Pincone in the introductory blog post or by visiting the website.
