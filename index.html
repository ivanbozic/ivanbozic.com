Ars Futura

I co-founded Ars Futura with a friend back in 2013 when I was 22 years old. Today, Ars Futura has 26 employees and we have worked with more than 30 clients from all around the world, building mobile and web apps.

Our clients range from small startups, established agencies to large corporations. We have worked with Activision Blizzard and their subsidiary Major League Gaming to bring esports to millions of people around the world. With a little help from us, Elemental Machines is revolutionizing science-based industries with powerful, data-driven insights. Evisort is out there on a mission to automate the entire contracting process. We're working with Harvard on technology that powers their innovation incubator, and Bevi even appeared on HBO's Silicon Valley.

You can find out more about Ars Futura and our clients at our website.
