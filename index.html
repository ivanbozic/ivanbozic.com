Pincone

Simply put, Pincone is a bookmarking tool. But it's a lot more than that! You can bookmark links. You can also subscribe to RSS feeds. And you can create unique email addresses and use them to subscribe to newsletters!

The next big thing I want to add to Pincone is the ability to add any type of content. Images, videos, files, notes. To hold everything and anything you find interesting.

I use Pincone every day and it's something that keeps me away from doomscrolling my day on socials. I am building it myself, for myself. And it's wonderful to see other people resonating with that and using it themselves.
